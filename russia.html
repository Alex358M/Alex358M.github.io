Today I will analyze the negative and positive aspects of the prison system in Russia. Is this system effective? What is better rehabilitation or punishment? Today we will try to analyze these and other features of the prison system in Russia.

Negative sides

Torture:¬†Torture is the main reason inmates commit suicide in Russian prison.

"The softest one could count on was the beating. Moreover, they tried to beat the genitals to deliver maximum suffering. In the sixth colony, they were shocked by attaching bare wires to the limbs, while the hands hung the person on the bars that separated the main cell of the punishment cell from the front door. I hung like that for 18 hours."

- The story of the prisoner Ruslan Lapytov to the journalist.

Beatings and other abuse of prisoners, according to Ruslan, were usual practice. Anything could be the reason for torture, be it a minor violation of the regime, a refusal to give money, or a complaint about detention conditions.

In addition to physical suffering, inmates in Russian prisons also experienced psychological pressure. The prison guards turn on a musical composition or cartoon songs at full volume and play it continuously all day. After years of such bullying and torture, it is unlikely that a person will live everyday life and become part of society again.

Once released, the stereotype always haunts them that either losers or dangerous people go to prison. They try to hide their past, and therefore always move from one low-paid job to another.
